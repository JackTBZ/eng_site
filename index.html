My name is Thanh Nhien. In this blog post, I would like to talk about my favourite means of transportation.

Means of Transportation

There are multiple ways to travel around Ho Chi Minh City. my favourite way to travel is by van.

My father owns a 2014 Chevrolet Express 1500 Research. It has four seats total, the two back seats being collapsable. The back of the van smells terrible because of the mysterious pee stains, blood stains and scratch marks. I wonder what my father uses this van for. Considering he goes to work in this van for entire days and sometimes even overnight. I'm sure he's not doing anything terrible.

But regardless, I like to sleep there on long trips. After all, it's what I use to go to school every day! It's a 4-wheeler, so traveling to school is very easy. It only takes me 15 minutes to go from home to school. Anywhere I go, I prefer going with this van over any other motorbike any day.

Travel Plan

For my next big trip, I will be going to the Philippines. I'm going there to meet my online friends.

I'm going to take a plane to Manila with my parents in January 2024. Since Tet lasts for 15 days, I'll be staying there for 10 days.

Conclusion

There are many ways to travel. But to each our preference. We have discussed my favourite means of transportation as well as my future travel plan.

My name is Thanh Nhien. I am a big fan of shopping. I love the excitement of finding new things and the satisfaction of getting a good deal. I used to shop exclusively in stores, but in recent years, I have become more and more of an online shopper. In this blog post, I will discuss my shopping habits. I will talk about where I shop, how often I shop, and what I usually buy. I will also talk about the factors I consider when I shop online, my payment methods, and my experiences with online and in-store shopping.

Shopping Location

I shop for a variety of items, including clothes, electronic devices, food, and home goods. I usually shop at a mix of stores and online retailers.

For clothes, I like to shop at department stores like Takashimaya and Parkson. I also like to shop at specialty stores like Zara and H&M. When I shop for clothes online, I usually use Shopee or Lazada.

For electronic devices, I like to shop at big-box stores like Vincom. I like to see and touch my electronics before purchasing them.

For food, I exclusively eat at McDonalds. No exceptions.

For home goods, I like to shop at department stores like Takashimaya. I also like to shop at online retailers like Shopee and Lazada.

Buying Online or at Shops

I used to prefer to shop in stores. I liked the experience of being able to try on clothes and see products in person. However, in recent years, I have become more and more comfortable shopping online.

There are a many reasons why I prefer shopping online. First, it is so much more convenient. I can shop from the comfort of my own home and I don't have to worry about traffic or gas. Last, it is a tiny bit more affordable. I can sometimes find better deals online, but most of them are still expensive.

How Often I shop

I shop online about once every few weeks. I also occasionally shop for food online, but I prefer to buy food in person as to avoid health risks.

What I Buy Online

I usually shop online for clothes, electronic devices, and home goods. When I shop for clothes online, I usually look for items that are on sale or that have good reviews. I also like to shop for clothes from brands that I trust.

When I shop for electronic devices online, I usually look for the best price and the best features. I also like to read reviews of the products to make sure that I am getting a good deal.

When I shop for home goods online, I usually look for items that are on sale or that are unique. I also like to shop for home goods from brands that I trust.

My favorite online store

My favorite website to shop online is Shopee. I like Shopee because it has a wide variety of products and it occasionally has good prices. I also like that Shopee is a Vietnamese company, so I don't have to worry about shipping costs or customs fees.

Factors in buying Online

I consider a few factors when I shop online.

First, I consider the price. I want to make sure that I am getting a good deal.

Second, I consider the reviews. I want to read reviews of the products to make sure that they are good quality.

Third, I consider the shipping costs. I want to make sure that the shipping costs are reasonable.

Fourth, I consider the return policy. I want to make sure that I can return the product if I am not satisfied.

My Payment Methods

I usually pay for my online purchases with a credit card. I like to use credit cards for its buyer protection. If I am not satisfied with a product, I can dispute the charge with my credit card company.

Bad experiences

I once ordered a gun for 40 million vnd. But then my seller stopped contacting me for some reason. I couldn't get ahold of them.

Another time, I ordered an electronic device that was defective. I tried to return said product but the retailer refused to accept the return.

Conclusion

Everyday life requires many purchases from different sources. Online is my go-to way.
